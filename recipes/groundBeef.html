Ground Beef

Description

How to cook and season ground beef to be used in other dishes, or eaten on its own.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Warm a pan over medium heat
  2. Put beef in pan and break it up with a spatula
  3. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder
  4. Once beef is brown, drain grease and serve
